# InvoiceGen: Invoice Details & Line Items

## Vibe
Detailed invoice view with itemized breakdown. Professional invoice document aesthetic with clear visual hierarchy. Brutalist typography and layout.

## Design System
- **Background**: `#0e0e0e` (dark black)
- **Header**: `#00fbfb` (cyan accent bar)
- **Primary Text**: White (#ffffff)
- **Secondary Text**: Gray (#999999)
- **Accent**: `#9fff88` (green for totals)
- **Dividers**: `#44a5ff` (subtle blue lines)

## Page Structure
**Header Section**:
- Client name and contact email
- Invoice number (INV-YYYY-NNNN)
- Invoice date and due date
- Status badge

**Line Items Table**:
- Description: service/product name
- Quantity: numeric amount
- Unit Price: price per item
- Total: line item total

**Summary Section**:
- Subtotal (right-aligned)
- Tax amount (right-aligned, highlighted)
- Total (right-aligned, large green text)

## Layout Details
- Structured invoice document format
- Clear client information at top
- Itemized table with alternating row shading
- Financial summary emphasized at bottom
- Professional business document appearance
- Clean spacing and hierarchy
- Tax calculation clearly visible
